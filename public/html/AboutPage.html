Study Helper allows students to save important study notes in topic, chapter and subject wise. These notes are stored in cloud using firebase real time database. It brings all the related references such as articles and you tube links in a single page. This includes voice input feature so important points can be added through voice while studying. The voice output feature will be useful for poor readers. It automatically reminds the students (through mail) based on their scheduled plan. Students can schedule their plans by selecting the respective time, date, month and year. When the deadline is close, reminder will be send to the student through auto generated mail.

Study Helper also includes authentication feature so that data of different users are stored separately and securely. Students need their email id and password to access their notes. Any time the students can add, modify or delete their notes. Using this website is simple and straight forward, which makes it more comfortable and time efficient. This website will be developed using HTML, CSS, Java Script, node JS, mongo DB and firebase cloud service.

While there are millions of tools on internet to spoil the lives of many students, why there shouldn’t be some tools that will bring back their lives to them. Modern technology has made us lazy to study hard. Usually when we start to study, we get bored and quit early. Students feel tired of downloading and organizing their study materials. Its difficult to make a collection of important notes for revising later, especially during exams. Also some students are unaware of their Assignment and quiz deadlines. These factors will gradually decrease their academic scores. These issues can be fixed by this website called ‘Study Helper’.

Students can save their study notes subject, chapter and even topic wise, which makes it easier for them to organize their notes. They can schedule their personal plan and deadlines, so that every time a deadline comes close, automatic reminder will be sent through mail. These habits will surely increase the student’s academic scores gradually.
